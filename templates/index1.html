Are you curious about world economy ranking?

Gain some insights on global economic inequality and find out the countries that are on the brink of the collapse

The open dataset World Development Indicators which is a collection of development indicators of the world, is made public by the World Bank to encourage reuse. This data ranges from 1960 - 2015 across 214 economies and covers a diverse range of indicators from various domains including Economy & Growth, Agriculture, Health, Education, Poverty etc, and presents the most recent and global development estimates.
